App Description

About Star Weaving

Connect stardust, weave constellations, explore the cosmos. Star Weaving is an original puzzle game where you draw paths on a star grid to trigger unique constellation effects — not your typical match-3. Master five constellation shapes, unlock lore in the Star Atlas, level up your Weaver rank, and chase high scores in Classic, Time Attack, or Endless mode.Help you pass the time
