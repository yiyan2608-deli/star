1. Introduction

Welcome to Star Weaving ("we", "us", "our", or the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, available on iOS devices through the Apple App Store.

Star Weaving is a casual puzzle game where players weave colorful threads to create star patterns and complete artistic designs. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

By downloading, installing, or using Star Weaving, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • User-Generated Content: Custom designs, patterns, or artwork you create and save within the App
  • Feedback and Support Requests: Information you provide when contacting our support team
  • Game Preferences: Settings you configure within the App (e.g., sound preferences, difficulty level, theme selections)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as ID for Advertising - IDFA, where permitted), and mobile network information
  • App Usage Data: Features accessed, levels completed, time spent in the App, gameplay statistics, in-app purchase history, and interaction patterns
  • Crash and Performance Data: Error logs, crash reports, and performance metrics to help us improve the App's stability
  • IP Address: Used for analytics, security, and to determine approximate geographic location (country/region level only) for content localization

2.3 Information from Third-Party Services

If you choose to connect third-party services to the App, we may receive information from those services:

  • Game Center: Your Game Center username, profile information, and leaderboard scores (if you opt in)
  • Social Media: If you choose to share your creations on social media platforms, we may receive confirmation of successful sharing

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Health or medical data
  • Precise geolocation data (GPS coordinates)
  • Personal contact information (name, email, phone number) unless you voluntarily provide it for support purposes
  • Biometric data
  • Financial account information (payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through App Store In-App Purchase)
